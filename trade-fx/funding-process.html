    Phase-1 is the first step of our trader evaluation. To trade an account connected to our firm’s capital, we need to make sure you can trade responsibly and manage risk appropriately. Phase-1 has reasonable rules, where the profit target is in balance with the drawdown allowed. We call these rules trading objectives. Traders have 30 days to complete this step. As soon as you meet all the Objectives of Phase-1, you can proceed to Phase-2. If you adhere to the risk management rules, finish with profit, but don’t meet the profit targets you are able to try again for free.
